About us

A platform that helps helps the users to travel any places within state or within country. The user can select the destination of his choice. If the the user select places within state then we will ask for his preference. If he wishes to travel within country we will suggest them best places that he can travel depending on the monsoon during his/her period of travel.

Various questions will be asked based on his preferences and based on his answer we will provide them the required details.It also asks user the mode of travel he wants to take(bus/flight/rail) and also approximate charge that will cost for the entire trip depending on their mode of travel, type of hotels they want to stay etc.Roadmap and GPS is used to search for in and around places which the user selects.
